IDEATHON 1.0

An ideathon is an innovative event where participants collaborate to generate and present creative ideas within a constrained timeframe, fostering problem-solving and entrepreneurship. Participants, often working in teams, brainstorm, develop, and pitch their ideas to a panel of judges or mentors.

GUIDELINES:-

* Each Team can have the maximum two members.
* 25th of November will be the last date of reistration.
* Teams can submit their ideas in pdf format in the Google form.
* Selected teams will be notified via e-mail for the final on 30th November.
